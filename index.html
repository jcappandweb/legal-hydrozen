Detailed Insights

Know your hydration habits inside out

HydroZen analyses your data week after week to surface patterns you wouldn't notice yourself — and gives you actionable nudges to improve.

  • Smart Insight — personalised alerts when you miss your goal or drink less on certain days
  • Consistency Score — see how regularly you hit your daily target
  • Hydration Trend — weekly and monthly charts of your average intake
  • Peak Hydration Time — discover when you naturally drink the most
  • Personal Performance — best streak, best day ever, success rate and more
  • Behavioral Patterns — average gap between drinks, reminder success rate

Hydration Lab

Find what works for you

Test different hydration strategies over 4, 5, 7 or 10 days. HydroZen compares your intake to your usual baseline and shows you exactly what worked best — with charts, statistics and a personalised recommendation.

Start an experiment, follow it for 5, 7 or 10 days, and discover your ideal hydration habit.

  • Sparkling Water — does switching to sparkling increase your intake?
  • Ice Cold Water — many people drink significantly more when it's chilled
  • One Big Bottle — fewer refills, always aware of how much is left
  • Always Visible — keep your bottle in plain sight at all times
  • Infused Water — add lemon, cucumber or mint for natural flavour
  • Morning Ritual — drink a full glass first thing before coffee or tea
  • Before Every Meal — drink a glass of water before each meal to boost your daily intake
